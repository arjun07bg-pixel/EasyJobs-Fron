Sales & Marketing Career Path

Entry Level (0-2 Years)

Sales Associate or Marketing Coordinator. Learn fundamentals of customer engagement and campaign execution. ₹2.5–5 LPA.

Mid Level (2-5 Years)

Sales Executive or Marketing Manager. Handle campaigns and client accounts independently. ₹5–10 LPA.

Senior Level (5–10 Years)

Senior Manager or Regional Sales Head. Lead teams and drive strategic initiatives. ₹10–18 LPA.

Expert Level (10+ Years)

Director of Sales/Marketing or CMO. Oversee entire departments and business growth strategies. ₹18–35+ LPA.
